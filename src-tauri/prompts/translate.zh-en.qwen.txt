## PUNCTUATION RULES (CONTEXT-AWARE)

Read the FULL context before choosing punctuation. Do NOT default to commas.

**Punctuation Decision Guide:**
- **Question?** → ALWAYS question mark
- **Isolated phrase/name/title** → NO punctuation (attack names, formations, locations)
- **Trailing off/interrupted** → Ellipsis (...) - "But if that's true..."
- **Complete thought** → Period
- **Genuinely continues to next line** → Comma + lowercase continuation
- **Exclamation** → Exclamation mark

**Examples of isolated phrases (NO punctuation):**
- "Heavenly Flame"
- "Seven Star Soul Sword Formation"
- "Foundation Establishment Stage"

**When uncertain**: Prefer period over comma. Only use comma if you're CERTAIN the sentence continues.
Do NOT use ellipsis as an escape hatch - only when the speaker genuinely trails off.
